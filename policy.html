Return & Replace Policy

At DOOM, we are committed to ensuring your satisfaction with every purchase. Our Return and Replace Policy is designed to offer a hassle-free experience, reflecting our confidence in the quality of our products and our dedication to customer happiness.

Policy Details:

  • Eligibility: Items can be returned or replaced within 14 days of receiving the order, provided they are unworn, unwashed, and in their original condition with tags intact.
  • Process: To initiate a return or replacement, simply contact our customer service team via email, phone, or our website. They will guide you through the process.
  • Refunds: Once the returned item is received and inspected, refunds will be processed promptly through your preferred method of payment.
  • Exceptions: Items on sale or under specific categories may not be eligible for returns or replacements. Details are mentioned on product pages.
